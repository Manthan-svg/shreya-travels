A Legacy of Trust, Built Over Three Decades

Founded in 1995, Shreya Travels started as a small, humble ticketing counter in Dighi, Pune, with a vision to make travel accessible and hassle-free for everyone.

Over the past 30 years, we have grown into a full-service travel agency, offering a comprehensive suite of services including domestic and international flight bookings, visa assistance, passport services, curated holiday packages, and reliable car rentals.

What remains unchanged is our commitment to family values and the trust we have built within our community. Having proudly served over 10,000 happy customers, our approach is deeply rooted in personal attention, ensuring every trip is meticulously planned and seamlessly executed by our founder, Avinash Lonare.
